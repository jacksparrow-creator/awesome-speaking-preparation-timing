## Speaking Time Calculators

Tools to convert word count to speaking time and plan your speech structure.

* [Speaking Time Calculator](https://speakingtimecalculator.com) - Multi-section speech management with personal WPM measurement. Features 8 speaking speed categories from slow (100 WPM) to fast auctioneer (250 WPM). Privacy-focused with no text storage. 

---

## Presentation Timers

Discrete timing tools with haptic feedback to keep you on track without distracting your audience.

* [Pro Speaker Presentation Timer](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/pro-speaker-presentation-timer/id1059920305) - iOS timer with Apple Watch support. Features tactile vibrations, discrete alerts, and section-by-section timing. **Paid**
* [Silent Presentation Timer](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=io.github.timopomer.silenttimer) - Android timer with Wear OS support. Pocket vibrations, color phases, background operation, no ads. **Free**
* [Speech Timer](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/speech-timer/id1602569287) - iOS timer with accelerometer-based flag signals, speech history export, and pre-configured speech types. **Paid**
* [Toastmasters Timer](https://www.toastmasters.org/membership/member-resources/apps) - Official Toastmasters timing app with color flags (green/yellow/red) and speech type presets. **Free/Paid versions**

## Teleprompters with Timing

Scripted delivery tools that help you maintain pace while reading.

* [PromptSmart Pro](https://promptsmart.com) - iOS/Android teleprompter with VoiceTrack auto-scroll technology. Adjusts speed to your voice in real-time. **Paid**
* [Virtual Teleprompter](https://virtualteleprompter.com) - Windows/Mac teleprompter with integrated timer and word counter. Features transparency control and multi-window support. **Paid**
* [Teleprompter Pro](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/teleprompter-pro-recording/id1455073216) - iOS/Mac teleprompter with timer feature, adjustable speed, and mirror mode. **Freemium**
* [VTP Apps Virtual Teleprompter](https://www.vtpapps.com/virtual-teleprompter) - Windows/Mac teleprompter with countdown timer, bookmarking, and multi-platform support. **Paid**

## AI Speech Coaches

Real-time feedback apps that analyze your pace, fillers, and clarity.

* [Orai](https://www.orai.com) - iOS/Android app for pace, filler words, clarity, and confidence tracking. Includes personalized lessons and daily practice. **Freemium**
* [Speeko](https://www.speeko.co) - iOS/Mac app analyzing pace, intonation, articulation, and filler words. Features activity library and progress tracking. **Freemium**
* [Yoodli](https://yoodli.ai) - Web-based AI speech coach with video recording library, pace analysis, and optional human coaching integration. **Freemium**
* [Poised](https://www.poised.com) - Real-time communication coach for Zoom/Meet. Provides live pace feedback and extensive resource library. **Freemium**
* [Microsoft Speaker Coach](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/office/rehearse-your-slide-show-with-speaker-coach-cd7fc941-5c3b-498c-a225-83ef3f65f07b) - Integrated in PowerPoint and web. Analyzes timing, pace, pitch, fillers, and generates rehearsal reports. **Free with Microsoft account**
* [Tempo](https://www.trytempo.ai) - Mac app for real-time pace monitoring, burst detection, and filler word tracking. Supports 30+ languages. **Freemium**
* [AcePace](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/acepace/id6738673287) - iOS/Android app developed by George Mason University. Real-time WPM tracking with visual feedback. **Free**

## Pace Training Tools

Metronomes and monitors to help you master your speaking rhythm.

* [Metronome Beats](https://www.stonekick.com/metronomebeats) - iOS/Android metronome with visual beat indicators. Set to 140-170 BPM for optimal speaking pace training. **Free**
* [Pro Metronome](https://www.eumlab.com/products/pro-metronome) - iOS/Android metronome with screen color changes, vibrations, and sound cues for pacing practice. **Freemium**
* [Ummo](https://ummoapp.com) - iOS app tracking "umms," pace, word power, and clarity. Custom word tracking for personalized improvement. **Paid**
* [LikeSo](https://likesoapp.com) - iOS app for speech fitness analysis with optimal pacing detection. Tracks "like" and "so" usage. **Paid**
* [VirtualSpeech Speaking Pace Exercise](https://virtualspeech.com/blog/speaking-pace-exercise) - Web-based tool with graph visualization of speaking pace. Practice at set speeds with 30+ second recordings. **Free**
* [TypingMaster Speech Speed Test](https://www.typingmaster.com/speech-speed-test) - Browser-based microphone test for measuring WPM. **Free**

## Filler Word Detectors

Tools to identify and eliminate crutch words from your vocabulary.

* [Fillerbuster](https://fillerbuster.app) - iOS/Android app detecting "um" and "ah" with speech recognition analysis and progress tracking. **Free**
* [SpeakFlare](https://speakflare.com) - Web-based tool for Zoom/Meet calls. Detects fillers across 50+ languages with real-time feedback. **Freemium**
* *Also see AI Speech Coaches above (Orai, Speeko, Poised) for integrated filler detection.*

---

## Recording & Review Tools

Capture and analyze your practice sessions.

* [Rev Voice Recorder](https://www.rev.com/voice-recorder) - iOS/Android high-quality recorder with human transcription service integration. **Freemium**
* [Vocaroo](https://vocaroo.com) - Simple web-based recording with shareable links. No registration required. **Free**
* [Sanako Web Recorder](https://www.sanako.com/web-recorder) - Chrome-based two-track recorder for master track + response practice. **Free**
* [Voice Analyst](https://www.speechtools.co/voice-analyst) - iOS/Android app with pitch and volume analysis, visual feedback, and recording save functionality. **Paid**

## Vocal Preparation

Warm up your voice before speaking.

* [Vocalizer](https://www.vocalizer.app) - iOS/Android app with 40+ vocal exercises, range detection, and customizable warm-up routines. **Free**
* [VocalPro](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/vocalpro/id123456789) - iOS app with specialized vocal warm-ups and custom routine creation for speakers. **Free**

## Integrated Rehearsal Suites

All-in-one environments for comprehensive practice.

* [Microsoft Speaker Coach](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/office/rehearse-your-slide-show-with-speaker-coach-cd7fc941-5c3b-498c-a225-83ef3f65f07b) - PowerPoint integration with timing, pace, pitch, filler word analysis, and detailed rehearsal reports. **Free with Microsoft 365**
* [VirtualSpeech](https://virtualspeech.com) - Web and VR-based practice with virtual environments, pace measurement graphs, and audience simulation. **Freemium**
* [Prezi Present](https://prezi.com) - Presentation software with built-in rehearsal mode and timing analytics. **Freemium**
* [Google Slides Presenter View](https://support.google.com/docs/answer/7212376) - Built-in speaker notes and timer for Google Slides presentations. **Free**
